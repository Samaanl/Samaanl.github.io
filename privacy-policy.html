Tab Saver Privacy Policy

How we handle your data

Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Tab Saver ("we", "our", or "extension") collects, uses, and protects any information when you use our browser extension. We are committed to ensuring that your privacy is protected and that you understand how your data is being used.

Information We Collect

Tab Saver is designed to save and restore your browsing sessions with minimal data collection. We only collect the following information:

  • Tab URLs: We save the URLs of tabs you choose to save in a group
  • Tab Titles: We save the titles of your tabs to help you identify them
  • Favicons: We save favicon URLs to display tab icons in the interface
  • Scroll Positions: We save the scroll position of web pages to restore your exact place when reopening tabs
  • User Preferences: We save your extension preferences, like dark/light mode settings

All of this information is necessary for the core functionality of the extension â€” saving and restoring your tab groups with the proper context.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used exclusively for:

  • Saving your tab groups for later retrieval
  • Restoring your saved tabs when you request them
  • Maintaining the proper scroll position when tabs are restored
  • Preserving your extension preferences

We do not analyze, process, or use your data for any other purposes beyond providing the Tab Saver functionality.

Where Your Data is Stored

All data collected by Tab Saver is stored locally in your browser using the Chrome Storage API. Your data:

  • Never leaves your device
  • Is not transmitted to any servers
  • Is not shared with third parties
  • Is only accessible from your browser

This means that your browsing data stays private and remains under your control at all times.

Data Retention

Tab Saver retains your saved tab groups and preferences as long as the extension is installed. If you remove the extension, all stored data will be deleted. You can also manually delete specific tab groups from within the extension interface.

Your Rights

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you have complete control over your data. You can:

  • View all saved tab groups through the extension interface
  • Delete any tab group at any time
  • Clear all data by uninstalling the extension

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted in an updated version of the extension, and the "Last Updated" date at the bottom of this Privacy Policy will be revised.
